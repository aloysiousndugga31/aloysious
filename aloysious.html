About Me

Hello! I'm Ndugga Aloysious, a student at Baptist who discovered a deep passion for financial markets, especially Forex trading. My academic journey and personal values have played a big role in shaping my approach to learning, risk-taking, and discipline in trading.

My Journey Into Forex Trading

My journey began during a quiet semester when I started exploring online opportunities to build financial freedom. After countless YouTube videos, demo trading accounts, and studying market psychology, I realized that Forex wasn't just about money—it was about mindset, discipline, and patience.

Coming from a Baptist background, I grounded my strategy in principles of integrity and stewardship. Each trade became a lesson in resilience. I made mistakes, learned from losses, and slowly built a strategy that worked for me.

Today, I'm not just trading—I’m growing, reflecting, and helping others avoid the mistakes I made early on. This site is where I’ll share my experiences, insights, and hopefully inspire others on a similar path.
